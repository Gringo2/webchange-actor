Troubleshooting & FAQ

Common solutions and answers for production monitoring with SWIM.

❓ I'm getting "Change Detected" but the page looks the same.

This is usually due to Invisible HTML shifts (e.g., hidden SEO meta tags or dynamic script IDs). While the Preprocessor removes 99% of this, some sites use custom attributes.

Solution: Check the #debug metadata in your dataset. It will show you exactly which hidden tags changed.

❓ The Actor is being blocked by a website.

Modern sites use sophisticated anti-scraping. Direct requests often get blocked with a 403 or 407 error.

Solution: Enable proxyConfiguration in your settings. Using Apify's Residential proxies is highly recommended for e-commerce sites.

❓ Visual Proof screenshots are showing the wrong area.

SWIM tries to guess the best area to screenshot based on your preset, but sometimes layouts are too complex.

Solution: Provide a custom cssSelector in the input. SWIM will precisely target that element for its Visual Proof.

Common Error Codes

SNAPSHOT_NOT_FOUND: This is normal for your first run. SWIM is just creating a baseline.

PROXY_LIMIT_EXCEEDED: You have run out of proxy credits on your Apify account.

AI_KEY_INVALID: Your OpenAI or Anthropic key is expired or incorrect.
